Pig farming with high welfare for animals and employees

At Volsgaard, since 1997 we have been breeding with Danish landrace and since then we have been breeding with Duroc and Landrace. Volsgaard's customers include both Danish and foreign pig producers and KS stations. We have customers both locally in Central and Western Jutland, and in the rest of Denmark.

Responsible for the holdings is Tommy Volsgaard, who has more than 25 years of experience in the industry. He and Volsgaard's employees ensure a common thread in work processes and provide great well-being for the animals. In addition to pig breeding and propagation, Volsgaard also runs 300 hectares of plant breeding with seeds and grains.

Among other things, we make sure that the breeding animals walk on a large and fixed floor area, which is good for their hooves. In addition, the breeding animals receive ample amounts of straw every day as well as home-made feed, which results in a pig breeding consisting of strong and healthy breeding animals. In our pig breeding, we also have close contact with the breeding animals every day to avoid human-shy breeding animals.

We breed pigs near Holstebro and Herning, where the focus is on a good local environment and quality feed. We offer good and healthy pig farming We deliver high-quality pig breeding, and you can therefore safely rely on being delivered both happy and healthy breeding animals. The breeding animals thus get used to people and behave calmly in their presence. With breeding animals from our pig breeding, you will therefore also get calm breeding animals in your herd.
